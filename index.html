Why EFAK?

Compatibility

Considering that different versions of Kafka design will be different, EFAK designed and implemented compatible new and old versions!

More Efficiency

For easier management and monitoring of topic, then deployment takes only a few minutes!

User-Friendly Dashboard

EFAK could present multi graph, including cluster & topic dashboard!

Open Source

EFAK is 100% open source and community-driven.
All components are available under the Apache 2 License on GitHub.
